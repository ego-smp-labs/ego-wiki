üåü Introduction to EgoSMP

What is Ego?

Ego is a PvP evolution system where you gain Tier (level 0-9) by killing other players and using their Ego Fragments.

Why play?

Higher Tier = More power! You receive bonus HP, damage, and unlock special skills.

What happens when I die?

You reset to Tier 1 (if awakened) or stay at Tier 0.

What is Anti-Farming?

Players Tier 2+ cannot farm Ego from Tier 0 players.

Mace Synergy?
