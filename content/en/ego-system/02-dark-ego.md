# Dark Ego

## What is Dark Ego?

A **corrupted** version of the Ego Fragment. Extremely rare and extremely dangerous.

## How to Obtain

- **Drop chance**: **5%** when killing a **Tier 5+** player
- Standard Ego Fragments drop at **95%** chance from Tier 5+ kills
- Below Tier 5, only normal Ego Fragments drop
