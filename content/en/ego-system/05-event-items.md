# Event Items

Beyond the permanent Ego Shards and Sin Hearts, the Ego world occasionally births strange entities through special events. These are highly contested items.

---

<ItemCard id="betrayer-heart" name="Betrayer's Heart" image="/wiki/items/the_betrayer_heart.gif" rarity="mythic">

> *"Its heartbeat is a death knell. You chose to betray the world, 
> now watch what the world does to tear it from your hands."*

The Betrayer's Heart cannot be forged or found normally. It is the center of the **"Betrayer's Blood Hunt"** event.

- **Ownership Mechanism**: 
  - Automatically equips to the **Off-hand** when picked up.
  - **Cannot be dropped**, cannot be placed in chests or any other container.
  - The holder's location is always **public to the whole server** via BossBar and the Glowing effect.
  - The holder emits a pulsing heartbeat sound, signaling the presence of death.

- **Curse & Blessing**: 
  - The holder receives great power but simultaneously becomes the #1 hunting target.
  - If the holder logs out, the heart will drop at their location.
  - When slain, the heart drops for others to claim.

</ItemCard>
