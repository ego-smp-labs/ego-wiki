# How to Play

If you are new to EgoSMP, here is the most important thing to understand:

**When a player is killed, their Ego drops.
The killer or whoever picks it up can absorb that Ego to progress their power.**

In short:

**getting killed = dropping Ego**
**absorbing Ego = ranking up**

## What is your true goal?

Your goal is not just to survive.

Your goals are:

1. Survive long enough to get geared
2. Engage in fights when you have the advantage
3. Kill others to take their **Ego**
4. Absorb **Ego** to progress
5. Reach the next **Tier** and unlock new powers

Thus, if you just farm and never step into the PvP loop, you will grow very slowly.
But if you rush into PvP too early, you become "free Ego".

This server does not reward those who risk recklessly.
It rewards those who take **calculated risks**.

## What is a Tier?

**Tier** is your progression level in EgoSMP.

The higher the Tier, the more you:

- become stronger
- have more choices
- gain access to new mechanics, skills, or build paths

Simply put:

- Low Tier: still green, easy to hunt
- High Tier: starting to become a threat

So when you meet a player who is vastly stronger, it might not just be god-like aim.
It's because they have absorbed enough Egos to leap far ahead of you.

![Tier Evolution](/images/general/tier-evolution.png)

## So how should beginners play?

The safest path is:

1. Loot fast, get food first
2. Get basic armor as soon as possible
3. Avoid fighting when you don't understand the system yet
4. Observe other players, see who is strong
5. Only engage in PvP when you can win or escape
6. Read the system pages carefully before going all-in

Do not underestimate the "read the wiki" step.
In EgoSMP, lacking information is more dangerous than lacking iron.

## How is PvP different here?

PvP in EgoSMP is not a side feature.
It is the main engine of progression.

When you kill someone, you don't just eliminate them from the fight momentarily.
You can also take the **Ego** they traded their time, resources, and life to build up.

This means every kill has real value.
That is what makes combat here much more intense than normal survival.

## What do you lose when you die?

Depending on the specific mechanics of the server, upon death you might:

- Lose your items
- Lose your standing
- Have your progression blocked
- Most importantly: drop your **Ego** for someone else to absorb

To be blunt:

**losing a fight in EgoSMP is not just losing a combat.
It might be you personally helping your opponent step onto the next Tier.**
