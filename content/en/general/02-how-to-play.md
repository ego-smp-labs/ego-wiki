# How to Play

## Step 1: Get Ego Fragments

- **Kill other players** - they will drop Ego Fragments
- ⏱️ Cooldown: 20-25 minutes per victim

## Step 2: Use the Fragment

- Hold the Ego Fragment
- **Right Click** to activate

## Step 3: Risk Assessment

| Chance | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| 5% | Instant death |
| 5% | Full heal (blessed) |
| 90% | Chaos State (10-15 seconds) |

## Chaos State

- Poison, Nausea, Darkness effects
- Inventory is scrambled
- **Survive = Tier Up!**
