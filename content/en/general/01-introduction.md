# Introduction to EgoSMP

Ego is a PvP evolution system where you gain **Tier** (level 0-9) by defeating other players and using their **Ego Fragments**.

## Why Play?

- Higher Tier = More powerful!
- Gain bonus health and damage
- Unlock special abilities
- But beware - death has consequences!

## Getting Started

1. **Kill other players** to make them drop Ego Fragments
2. **Pick up and use** with Right Click
3. **Survive the Chaos State** to increase your Tier!
