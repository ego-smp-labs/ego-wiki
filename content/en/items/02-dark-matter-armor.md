# Dark Matter Armor

> [!WARNING]
> This armor consumes the wearer's XP to repair itself.

The **Dark Matter Armor** is a Tier 4 protection set in Ego SMP.

## Crafting
Crafted using **Dark Matter Ingots** and **Void Essence**.

| Piece | Defense | Durability | Special Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Helmet | 4 | 500 | Night Vision |
| Chestplate | 9 | 800 | Resistance I |
| Leggings | 7 | 750 | Speed I |
| Boots | 4 | 400 | No Fall Damage |

## Set Bonus: "Void Walker"
When wearing the full set, you become invisible to Endermen.
