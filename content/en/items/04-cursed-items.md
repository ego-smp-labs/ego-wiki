# Cursed Items

The **Cursed Crafting Table** unlocks the ability to forge dark and forbidden items. These items often come with a heavy price.

## Cursed Crafting Table

A special crafting station required to forge **Dark Ego** and **Chaos Mace**.

### Recipe
- **Ingredients**:
  - 1x **Dark Ego Soul** (Top Middle)
  - 1x **Crafting Table** (Center)
  - 1x **Netherite Ingot** (Bottom Middle)
  - 2x **Obsidian** (Left/Right Middle)
- **Output**: Cursed Crafting Table

> **Usage**: Place it down and right-click to open the Cursed Forging GUI.

---

## Crafting Components

### Dark Ego Soul
- **Description**: A fragment of a soul tainted by the abyss.
- **Drop**: Rare drop or special event item.
- **Usage**: Used to craft the **Cursed Crafting Table**.

### Tainted Ego
- **Description**: An Ego Fragment that has lost its purity.
- **Recipe**: **Ego Fragment** + **Netherite Ingot** (Shapeless).
- **Usage**: The core ingredient for forging **Dark Ego**.

### Heavy Core
- **Description**: A dense, heavy core found in the deep.
- **Usage**: The crushing heart of the **Chaos Mace**.
