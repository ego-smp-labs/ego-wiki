# Mace of Chaos — "The Glitch"

> ☠️ A corrupted weapon born from the void. Wielding it comes at a **terrible cost**.

## Stats

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Material | Mace |
| Custom Model | `2002` |
| Kill Display | `█▓▒░ GLITCH ░▒▓█` (obfuscated) |

## Crafting Recipe

```
[N]  [H]  [N]
[H]  [M]  [H]
[N]  [W]  [N]
```

| Slot | Material | Quantity |
|------|----------|---------|
| **N** | Netherite Ingot | ×4 |
| **H** | Heavy Core | ×3 |
| **M** | Mace (base) | ×1 |
| **W** | Wither Rose | ×1 |

## ⚠️ Risk: Self-Curse on Craft/Pickup

The moment you craft or pick up the Mace of Chaos:

| Effect | Duration |
|--------|----------|
| **Wither** | 10 seconds |
| **Inventory Shuffle** | 10 seconds (shuffled every 5 ticks) |

> You WILL take damage just from picking it up!

## Combat Effects

### Inventory Shuffle (on hit)
- **20% chance** per hit to shuffle the victim's inventory
- Duration: 5 seconds
- Shuffle interval: every 5 ticks

### Glitch Kill Name
When you kill someone with the Mace of Chaos, the kill message shows a **glitched/obfuscated name** — hiding the killer's identity.

### Permanent Effects
- **Glowing**: Enabled (everyone can see you)
- **Chat Announce**: Server broadcasts when the Mace is used in combat
